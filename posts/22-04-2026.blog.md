# Commiting to ideas
After much thinking of how each ideas are developed we've decided to commit on the recovery web app idea. The reason for this being we had the clearest vision on what it could be and how it could look like. The idea is that it will be able to track your personal recovery from an injury, while giving you suggestions of past blogs from previous users talking about their injuries. We believe the web app has a strong core function of recovery tracking while being able to have a unique social functions with the referencing of past experiences.
