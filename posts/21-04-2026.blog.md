# Concepting and Choosing a Problem Space
I'm currently deciding on different community spaces and and possible problems for key features, for example:<br>

University Students: First year university students often reports the new learning structure and environment being overwhelming leading to students falling behind and feeling discouraged early on. However this can be remedied by the company of other students willing to help as well as educators, I plan on creating a system for struggling students to find study partners online to help aid in the learning process of higher education.

Artists: The art industry while full of helpful individuals ready to reward people with talent, people still find trouble finding entry level positions to start out and establish their career. Thought present in other websites such as, Reddit forums, Artstation, Linkedin, etc. Entry level art positions are more scarcely dispersed compared to most jobs, and existing websites don't have specific methods of funnelling access to jobs to young artists. My idea is to create a hub where artist of all genres looking to start their career can safely rely on to get introduced to established people, and opportunities.

I've not really leaning towards a singular idea, and more ideas could come as the iterating continues.
